# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students, educators, and curious developers learning how convolutional neural
networks transform image tensors into class probabilities.

## Product Purpose

Turn a real browser-run CNN into a clear, interactive mathematical story.
Success means a learner can draw a digit, follow each transformation, and
understand which views are real model data and which are labeled educational
simplifications.

## Brand Personality

Precise, curious, calm, cinematic.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI dashboards covered in purple gradients and glass cards
- Decorative motion that competes with the lesson
- Fake activations or unlabeled simplified diagrams
- Copying 3Blue1Brown branding, assets, characters, fonts, or visual identity

## Design Principles

- Make the current mathematical idea the visual focus.
- Use color to encode meaning, not decoration.
- Keep real model evidence visibly distinct from simplified teaching views.
- Let motion explain transformations and remain optional.
- Preserve a clear path from drawing to prediction on every device.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, touch-friendly
targets, responsive layouts, and a complete reduced-motion path.
